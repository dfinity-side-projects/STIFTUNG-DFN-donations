Create your DFN address seed, and store it very safely

This application does not store your DFN seed. You must securely maintain this yourself.

Your DFN (DFINITY token) seed is a string of random words and is shown above. You must have this seed to access your DFN later. This application does not store the seed for security reasons, and only keeps the derived keys it needs. Therefore, it is ESSENTIAL that you take a copy of your seed right now and store it safely or YOU WILL LOSE ALL ACCESS TO YOUR DFN.

Security Notes

We recommend that you create physical, printed copies of your seed. For example, print out the seed onto paper and put it into a fireproof safe. If you keep electronic copies of your seed, make sure they are secure and nobody else can access them. For example, store an electronic seed inside a Mac OS X encrypted file.

Important Notes

This special "ETH forwarding address" was created from your DFN seed.

Whenever this application detects that the "ETH forwarding address" has received ETH, it forwards the ETH to the Stiftung DFINITY smart contracts that are collecting donations.

Stiftung DFINITY records the value donated in Swiss Francs (CHF) and uses this to calculate recommendations for genesis dfinities. Its systems are regularly updated with the CHF to ETH exchange rate for this purpose.

When a total of 1M CHF in value has been received, Stiftung DFINITY will stop accepting further ETH donations. The application will allow you to withdraw any ETH you sent to the forwarding address after the limit was reached.

Note that there is a small Ethereum gas cost associated with each donation, and this is deducted from the amount.

OVERVIEW OF DONATING TO DFINITY STIFTUNG

The DFINITY Network and DFN

Research, designs, cryptography and proof-of-concept software has been created and proposed in relation to an open and decentralized cloud computing platform (the “DFINTY Network”) that involves advanced blockchain technology and techniques. It is necessary for the DFINITY Network to host special cryptographic digital information units (“DFN”) that mediate participation in its functions and access to its resources. At the current time, the DFINITY Network concept involves the DFN participation tokens enabling and mediating participation in four ways:

  • Deploying and running software code.
  • Creating “mining identities” that enable participation in mining the network using special client software.
  • Creating “neurons” that enable participation in decentralized governance created by a “Blockchain Nervous System” .
  • Connecting private cloud compute platforms created using DFINITY technology to the DFINITY Network so the software they host can interact with public systems.

The DFINITY Foundation

Numerous parties and organizations (“Participants”) wish for the DFINITY Network to come into existence so that they can build applications on a decentralized compute cloud platform, so they can create private compute clouds using its technology that that might be connected to the DFINITY Network, so they can run computations using the software that other parties have installed on the DFINITY Network, or so that they can participate in mining the network or participate in its governance.

To support the development of the network’s protocols and technology, a not-for-profit foundation has been created (the “Foundation”) in Zug, Switzerland, that is tax exempt. The Foundation will be funded by donations. Participants may wish to make donations to the Foundation to support the pursuit of its objectives. The Foundation will accept donations from Participants in three automated funding rounds performed using the Ethereum network and other mechanisms.

Collecting Donations and Allocating DFN

Since the network requires Participants to hold DFN in order to function, donations to the Foundation will be recorded in a special smart contract hosted on Ethereum (the “FDC”, or “Foundation Donations Contract”).  The hope of the Foundation is that the FDC will define the initial allocations of DFN made to Participants. Therefore when the Foundation judges that special client software (the “Client”) required to create the network is sufficiently mature, and other factors make launch of the network possible, the Foundation will recommend that the FDC is used as part of an initial state (“Genesis State”) of the DFINITY Network.

It is important to understand that an open decentralized network results from the activities of independent participants performing mining activities around the world. Because such “miners” are independent, the Foundation cannot guarantee that its recommendation to use the FDC as the basis of initial allocations of DFN in the Genesis State will be adopted. It is merely hoped that this will be the case because miners generally have an incentive to agree on some logical Genesis State that reflects contributions made to the project.

Donations (“Donations”) will be collected from donors (“Donors”) in ETH/”ether”, BTC/”bitcoin” and CHF/”Swiss francs”. The FDC will record the nominal value of each Donation in CHF. The current exchange rate used to calculate the nominal value of a Donation is at the sole discretion of the Foundation. Furthermore, the value of a Donation recorded will be the product of the actual money value calculated using the exchange rate and a current multiplier (“Multiplier”) designed to reflect that earlier Donations are more valuable for the project.

The Foundation will make available an official software system that can enable Donors to make Donations. Only this system should be used to make Donations.

The Donation Collection Rounds

Donations will be collected in three rounds: The first round (“Bootstrap Round”) will be restricted to those who have already made early contributions to the project in the form of enabling technologies and other forms of support. The Foundation intends to recommend that Donations made in the Bootstrap Round are matched to allocations of DFN in the Genesis State that are equal to 20% of whatever total quantity of DFN is allocated. Thus if a total of 1000 DFN are allocated in the Genesis State, exactly 200 DFN will be divided amongst those Donors making Donations in the Bootstrap Round.

A “Seed Round” and “Main Round” in which anyone can make donations will follow the Bootstrap Round.  The intention is that the Main Round will be run after a beta version of the DFINITY Network has been launched that clearly showcases its technology. These rounds will last a maximum of six weeks, or will terminate 1 hour after they succeed in collecting a targeted amount of donations (“Funding Targets”) measured by their equivalent value in CHF/”Swiss francs”. The Seed Round has a Funding Target of 1,000,000 CHF and the Main Round has a Funding Target of 20,000,000 CHF. Because these funding rounds will continue to run for 1 hour after their Funding Targets have been met, they may collect more value than the Funding Targets.

The Foundation intends to recommend that 80% of the total DFN allocated in the Genesis State are allocated in consideration of Donations made in the Seed Round and Main Round. As a baseline, 10 DFN will be allocated for every 1 CHF in value that is recorded as donated by the FDC. During the Seed Round, the Multiplier will be 2.5X. During the Main Round, a different Multiplier will be applied in each successive week: 1.25X, 1.20X, 1.15X, 1.10X, 1.05X and 1.00X.

The Seed Round is expected to take place in December 2016. The Main Round is scheduled to take place on the 1st of March 2017, but can be delayed a maximum of three times for 1 month by the Foundation using a privileged call it can make into the FDC.

Restrictions and Warnings

Only those who (i) are interested in participating in the DFINITY Network in the prescribed ways, and (ii) wish to further the development of its technologies for philanthropic reasons and accept the project might fail completely should become Donors. The purpose of any DFN that the Foundation recommends are allocated in the Genesis State is to mediate participation in the DFINITY Network and it may cause harm to the network if these tokens are alternatively allocated to speculators. Furthermore, due to the ambitious aims of the DFINITY project described and the uncertain viability of developing a “decentralized cloud computing” platform generally any speculator making Donations with the intention of receiving DFN allocations they can resell for profit will probably make a loss on average.

Due to the uncertain nature of regulations in the United States, citizens and residents of the United States cannot make Donations at this time.
